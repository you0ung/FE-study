Sphynx

Sphynx is from the Cananda.
The Sphynx's has many wrinkles on its body.
Also, Sphynx is a short-haired breed.
It is characterized by its large ears and big eyes,
and it is sensitive to both cold and heat.

Turkish Angora

Turkish Angora is from the Angora in Turkey.
It is the most famous among white-haired cats,
with eye colors that can be blue, green, yellow.
The breed is particularly notable for its attractive
odd-eyed variation.

Siam

Siam is from Thiland. The origin of the Siam cat
name is the old name for Thailand.
Its physical features include sapphire-blue eyes
and a long tail that adds to its elegance.
